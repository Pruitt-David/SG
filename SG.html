About the Game

Natural Selection is a board game about Dominace, Sentience, or Elimination. Your a single cell organism working your way to becoming a sentient creature. Want to overpoulate the world through dominance? Become and Herbivore and overpopulate the planet. Want to be a sentient creature able to reason? Become an omnivore and gain abilities to help you survive longer to reach sentience. Want to elminate the competition? Become a carnivore and devour your opponets. With a deck of beautifully acrylic attack, defense, and event cards by our artist Kaleigh Quick, the endless combinations turn every game into a new game experience.

Cards

Abundance

A time of plenty for every species. Which increases your ability to evolve into a sentient creature.

Bitumen Seep

A dark substance usually associated with tar, pitch, and asphalt. Prehistoric animals would get stuck in this substance and struggle to get out only to get more trapped in the seep.

Eclipse

An event of obscuring the light from one celestial body by the passage of another between it and the observer. Causing a night like event in the middle of the day gives predators a once in a life time chance to catch its prey off guard.

Drought

A prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall; a shortage of water resulting from this. Which makes more animals more fearsome in such shortages.

High Tide

A state of the tide when it is at its highest level. High tides effect reproductive cycles of plants and animals. It provides a protection to smaller creatures.

Ice Age

A glacial episode during a past geological period. Which slows down your ability to evolve into a sentient creature.

Mating Season

The period during each year when a particular bird, animal, or fish procreates. Which increases your population.

Poleshift

A phenomenon where the earths magnetic poles shift causing massive changes to enviroments, and even causing creatures to migrate to new areas.

Providence

The protective care of God or Nature as a spirital power. Generally finding a new home that a creature can thrive in because of the abundance of resources available to them here over where they came from.

Volcanic Eruption

The sudden occurrence of a violent discharge of steam and volcanic material. Which decreases your ability to attack.

Game Play

Unlike most games, Natural Selection, has no disc or controller and it compatiable with most table tops. Featuring game play with 2 - 6 players.

Set-Up


  1. Start by placing the the game board in the center of all the players.
  2. Give each player a Species Chart Tracker.
  3. Give each player 5 color tokens of the same color as well as a pair of dice; one red and one blue.
  4. Have each player place one token of their color on the board at 20 population.
  5. Place a token for each player on the species chart starting at level 1.
  6. Place the Abundance Card from the Natural Selection Deck as the Current Event.
  7. Shuffle the Deck and deal 3 cards to every player.
  8. Place the blue and red crystals around the board within reach of each player.
  9. The oldest player goes first by drawing a card to start their turn.
